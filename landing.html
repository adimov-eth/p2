Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, transparent terms designed for corporate members.

What exactly is included in the 700,000 THB membership? ▾
Two named seats and 150 shared round credits per year. Prime covers the green fee at listed partner courses up to published ceilings; members pay cart & caddie at course rates.
Do weekends cost more credits? ▾
Yes. Weekdays deduct 1 credit per round; weekends and public holidays deduct 1.5 credits per round.
Can we bring guests? ▾
Guesting is allowed within monthly limits. Green fee is covered up to ceilings when booked under your membership; guest pays cart & caddie. Weekend guesting may require additional credits or surcharges.
How far in advance can we book? ▾
Typical booking windows are 7–14 days depending on course. Concierge will confirm or propose alternates within 2 hours.
What’s the no‑show policy? ▾
Cancellations inside 48 hours or no‑shows may deduct 0.5–1 credit per reserved slot to protect member access.
Which courses are covered? ▾
See the Courses & Coverage section for the current roster and course-by-course green fee ceilings.
