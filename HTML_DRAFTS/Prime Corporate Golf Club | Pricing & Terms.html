Your Prime Corporate Golf Club membership is subject to the following terms:

  • Membership Term: Membership is valid for 12 months from the date of activation.
  • Registered Users: Each corporate membership allows for two (2) named primary users. These individuals must be personnel of the member corporation.
  • Transfer of Rights: Transferring membership or changing a named user is permitted. A fee of ฿5,000 applies for each change. The new user must be an employee or director of the same corporation.

To ensure a premier experience for all members, please adhere to the following usage policies:

  • Annual Quota: Play is limited to a maximum of 144 rounds per year per membership.
  • Daily Limit: Play is limited to one round (18 holes) per day for each registered user or guest.
  • Guest Accompaniment: Registered members must accompany their guests at all times during play on the course.
  • Group Size: A single registered member can host up to 3 guests (totaling a group of 4). Both registered members can host up to 6 guests (totaling a group of 8).

All tee times must be arranged through your Personal Concierge. The following policies apply:

  • Booking Procedure: Contact your Personal Concierge to arrange all bookings. Bookings for premium 5-star courses should be made at least 14 days in advance.
  • Course Availability: If a member's first choice of course is unavailable, the member agrees to accept an alternative tee time at another available course within the network.
  • Cancellation Notice: To cancel or change a booking without penalty, you must notify your Personal Concierge at least 48 hours prior to your scheduled tee time.
  • Penalty for Late Cancellation/No-Show: A cancellation made less than 48 hours before the scheduled time, or failure to appear for a booking (no-show), will result in the deduction of one (1) round from your annual quota for each player booked.

Members and their guests are expected to uphold the highest standards of etiquette.

  • Course Regulations: All members and guests must strictly adhere to the rules, regulations, and etiquette of each individual golf course. This includes dress codes and pace of play.
  • Liability: Prime Corporate Golf Club is not liable for any personal injury or property damage that occurs on the premises of any partner golf course.
