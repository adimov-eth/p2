Prime Corporate Golf Club

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Prime Corporate Golf Club ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and disclose personal data for our corporate members, their designated users, and guests in compliance with Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) ("PDPA").

This policy applies to all our services, including the use of our LINE Mini-App.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data to provide our services:

  • Corporate Member Data: Company name, registered address, billing information, and contact details of the company representative.
  • Designated User Data: Full name, email address, phone number, and LINE User ID of the two individuals designated by the Corporate Member to use the membership.
  • Guest Data: Full name of any guest accompanying a Designated User.
  • Usage Data (LINE Mini-App):
    • Booking Information: History of courses booked, dates, tee times, and fellow players.
    • Activity Logs: Records of your interactions with the Mini-App, such as feature usage and digital invitation history.
    • NFC Check-in Data: Timestamps and location data from when you use the digital membership card for check-in at partner courses.
  • Communication Data: Records of correspondence when you contact our Personal Concierge service.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes, based on a lawful basis under the PDPA:

Purpose of Processing Type of Data Used Lawful Basis
To Provide and Manage Your Membership All categories Contractual Necessity
To Process Bookings & Facilitate Golf Rounds User, Guest, Usage Data Contractual Necessity
To Communicate with You User & Communication Data Contractual Necessity
To Improve Our Services and App Functionality Usage Data Legitimate Interest
To Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud All categories Legitimate Interest
To Send Marketing & Promotional Offers User Data Consent

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your personal data with the following parties when necessary:

  • Partner Golf Courses: To confirm your booking and identity upon arrival. We only share the minimum data required (e.g., names of players, tee time).
  • LINE Corporation: As our service is a LINE Mini-App, certain data is processed by LINE as the platform provider. We recommend you review LINE's own privacy policy.
  • IT Service Providers: We use third-party providers for services like cloud hosting and software maintenance who may have access to your data but are contractually obligated to protect it.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or government regulation.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or for the duration required by applicable laws (e.g., accounting and tax laws). Once your corporate membership is terminated and legal retention periods have expired, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

6. Security of Your Data

We have implemented robust technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or destruction. These measures include access controls, data encryption, and regular security assessments.

7. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the PDPA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data where it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request to limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data, particularly for direct marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we process data based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer using the details below.
