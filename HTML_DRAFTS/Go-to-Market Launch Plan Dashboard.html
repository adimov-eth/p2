Go-to-Market Launch Plan

Objective: Secure the first 10 "Founders' Circle" members.

Part 1: The Main Event

"The Founders' Circle Invitational"

An exclusive, invitation-only golf and networking event to give prospects a tangible taste of the Prime experience.

Key Details:

  • Concept: Half-day luxury, networking & business strategy.
  • Venue: Top-tier partner course (Siam Country Club, etc.).
  • Guest List: 20-25 hand-picked executives.

Event Schedule:

  • 08:00 AM: Arrival, Breakfast & Welcome Kit
  • 09:00 AM: High-Impact Presentation
  • 09:30 AM: Curated 9-Hole Round
  • 12:30 PM: Gourmet Networking Lunch
  • 02:00 PM: Event Conclusion & Departure Gift

Part 2: Supporting Digital Campaign (6-Week Timeline)

Weeks 1-2: Preparation

  • 1.Finalize Guest List: Select top 25 individuals from the target company list.
  • 2.Create RSVP Landing Page: Simple, elegant, on-brand single page with an RSVP form.
  • 3.Craft Email Sequence: Write a 3-part personalized email series (Invite, Follow-up, Confirmation).

Weeks 3-4: Initial Outreach

Personalized Email - Wave 1

Send initial invitations. CTA to the RSVP landing page.

LinkedIn Ad Campaign

Launch highly targeted ads to the guest list and lookalikes. CTA to "Request Your Invitation".

Week 5: Follow-Up

Personalized Email - Wave 2

Send a polite follow-up to non-respondents, highlighting key benefits.

Sales Team Direct Outreach

Begin personal follow-ups (phone, LinkedIn) to high-priority targets.

Week 6: Finalization

  • 1.Close RSVPs at the deadline.
  • 2.Send detailed final confirmation emails to all attendees.
  • 3.Finalize all event logistics with the partner course.

Part 3: Post-Event Follow-Up (Within 48 Hours)

1. Personalized "Thank You" Email

Send a genuine thank you from the head of sales to each attendee. Reference a specific conversation to build rapport.

2. Schedule Consultations

The primary call-to-action is to schedule a brief, 15-minute "Personalized Consultation" to discuss their company's specific goals and present the Founders' Circle agreement.
