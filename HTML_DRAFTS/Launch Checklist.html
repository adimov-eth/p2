Prime Corporate Golf Club

Product Launch Checklist

Phase 1: Legal & Administrative Foundation

0%
Draft a legally binding contract for corporate clients. Include clauses on payment, liability, confidentiality, renewal/cancellation policies, and dispute resolution. Have it reviewed by a lawyer.
Formalize contracts with all 80+ partner courses. Define terms for booking, payment, blackout dates, rules for members/guests, and SLAs.
Ensure the company is properly registered and secure adequate liability insurance.
Create a clear privacy policy for handling member data (especially for the LINE Mini-App) and ensure compliance with local data protection laws (PDPA).

Phase 2: Operations & Logistics

0%
  • Hire and train the Personal Concierge team.
  • Set up dedicated phone, email, and LINE Official Account channels.
  • Create a detailed internal playbook for all procedures.
Finalize development of all features (Digital Card, NFC, Portal, Tracking, Invites) and conduct rigorous user acceptance testing (UAT).
Set up a robust system to handle corporate invoicing, track payments, and manage settlements with partner courses.

Phase 3: Marketing & Sales

0%
Determine the final annual membership fee and consider any early-bird or founding member incentives.
  • Prepare a sales presentation deck and a one-page summary.
  • Train the sales team on the product, target audience, and key selling points.
Develop a list of target companies (law firms, financial institutions, tech companies, luxury brands) for initial sales outreach.
Plan a small-scale launch event or a targeted digital marketing campaign aimed at initial prospects.

Phase 4: Member Experience & Onboarding

0%
Create a premium physical package including a welcome letter, NFC membership cards, a "Getting Started" guide, and a list of top 10 courses.
Design and source a small collection of exclusive, high-quality branded merchandise (e.g., premium golf balls, polos, caps).
Develop a structured onboarding process, including a personal welcome call or email from their dedicated concierge.
